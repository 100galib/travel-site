Overview

This is a perfect 3 Night 4 Days long weekend tour package for customers visiting Andaman and Nicobar Islands. Visit the top-rated islands in the Andaman archipelago during your visit. Indulge in cruise rides and water activities during this 4 days tour to Andaman. The tour comes inclusive of stay, transfers, cruise rides and sightseeing. A personal coordinator is assigned to you during your trip who is available for any ground assistance. This weekend tour of the Andaman Islands covers the top two islands in the Andaman archipelago. Both Port Blair and Havelock Islands are covered during this trip. The top places covered in this tour are

  • Cellular Jail
  • Light and Sound Show
  • Corbyns Cove Beach
  • Radhanagar Beach
  • Elephant Beach
  • Kalapathar Beach

In addition, we can customize this trip further to add some amazing water activities at Havelock Island. Making sure you weekend trip to the islands is a memorable one.
